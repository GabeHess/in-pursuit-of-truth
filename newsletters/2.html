Chivalry: Relic Of The Past?


Gone are the days of the chivalrous gentleman holding the door for the fair lady. Gone, in fact, are the days of the chivalrous gentleman. What young man of the twenty-first century would think to lay bare his head in the presence of a lady—if ladies may still be said to walk this earth; to have survived the invasive horde of neo-feminists which daily floods us with a petulant storm of self-pity? Upon further reflection, what young man would even be aware that such borderline moronic behavior was once in vogue? Speaking as a young man, I have only seen such borderline moronic behavior in books and movies—old books and movies.
